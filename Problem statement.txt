Problem Statement
Insurance companies are taking more time to verify and process medical insurance claims.
Manual verification processes increase claim processing delays and operational workload.
Usually, insurance claim updates take around 7–15 days to complete.
There are mainly two types of medical insurance claims:
Cashless Claim
Reimbursement Claim
In a cashless claim, the insurance company directly pays the hospital expenses.
Cashless claim processing generally takes around 2–4 days for approval and settlement.
In a reimbursement claim, the customer first pays the hospital bill and later claims reimbursement from the insurance company.
Reimbursement claims usually take around 10–20 days for verification and payment processing.
Recently, there has been an increase in fraudulent activities in medical insurance claims.
In the initial stage, companies manually verify insurance policy IDs, hospital reports, and uploaded medical documents before processing claims.
These document verification and validation procedures consume significant time and may lead to human errors.
To solve these issues, the AI Claim Assistant automatically verifies reports, extracts claim information, generates summarized reports, reduces manual effort, speeds up claim processing, and minimizes human interaction.
