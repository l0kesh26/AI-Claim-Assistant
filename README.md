**AI Claim Assistant**
**1. Problem Statement**
The current insurance landscape faces several critical challenges:

Lengthy Processing Times: Verification and processing typically take 7–15 days.

Cashless Claims: Direct hospital payments take 2–4 days.

Reimbursement Claims: Manual filing takes 10–20 days.

**Increased Fraud**: A recent surge in fraudulent medical claims has made manual verification difficult.

**Manual Bottlenecks:** Initial stages (ID and document verification) are slow and prone to human error.
